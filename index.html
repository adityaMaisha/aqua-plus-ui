WHY ALKALINE

Why should we be concerned about pH levels?

Since most of the body is water-based (50-60%), the pH level has profound effects on all aspects of our body . Having a balanced pH level, helps our body support proper physiological functioning.  Some of the top benefits of having a balanced pH are :

Cellular function: It helps in proper function of the cells. If our bodies Ph levels (7.35 - 7.45) fall below the natural range, it can impair cellular activities including enzyme function, energy production and oxygen transport. 

Bone Health: Falling below the range, turns the body's environment acidic, which will lead to cancium being leached from the bones to neutralise the excess acid potentially weakening bones.

Digestive health: Imbalance in the body's pH levels can cause digestive issues which can lead to acid reflux or indigestion. 

Metabolic processes: pH levels can impact the body's ability to metabolize nutrients and regulate hormones, influencing energy levels, mood and overall health.

So what is the solution?

Drinking alkaline water from Aquaplus's BPA-free bottles can help in maintaining our bodies pH levels. Alkaline water has powerful antioxidant properties that help with better digestion, neutralises excess stomach acid, better bone health, improved energy and detoxification
